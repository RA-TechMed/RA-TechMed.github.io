About Me

I'm a current junior at Patrick Henry High School and this is my website! The purpose of this is to showcase pictures, quotes from people I worked with and projects I made. My plan is to pursue biomedical engineering in college. Throughout my time in highschool, I have completed two engineering pathways — Computer Science and Engineering Technology. Over the past three years, I've operated industrial machines, led a student club from the ground up, completed an selective environmental summer program, and served as a grant lead for a nonprofit. I love building things: whether that's a robot chassis, games using Java, or a community.
